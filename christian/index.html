Hey there, I’m Cris, a visual designer with a focus on crafting identities that are organic, playful, and unexpected.

My Bio:

My path to design was unconventional. After initial design coursework, I devoted a year to developing my drawing skills and growing my social media presence as an artist. During this time, I developed my drawing practice which continues to inform my design portfolio development. If you’d like to see my artwork, feel free to find it here:

While also developing my drawing practice, I worked with and lead teams in a variety of fields at a property management organization. This developed my skills as a solid communicator, team player, and leader. I coordinated/scheduled, organized, and provided excellent customer service with a friendly and helpful attitude, resulting in a 50% increase in client satisfaction. This professional experience continues to inform my professional practices and I am able to meet any new challenge with agility.

When I came back to school, I was reinvigorated when I worked on identity design projects. I view identity design as a form of storytelling that allows businesses to share their values and story. So, when I worked on my first rebrand project, it was my chance to help this business speak their truth and stand out from the crowd with my design skills.

Gaming is a burning passion of mine because of the immersive storytelling potential and the branching pathways of each story highlighting multiple users. This passion informs my design practice as I see each brief as a possibility to engage diverse audiences through creative storytelling tactics and impactful visuals. My goal is to work on unique identities within the gaming industry.
