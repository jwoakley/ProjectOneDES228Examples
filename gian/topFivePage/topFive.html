Top Fives

Introduction:

During his service with the US Navy, Gian was able to travel to different countries. In this project, he will share the photos he took while traveling, along with a few from his experiences.

Places I Have Been

Kyoto, Japan

Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

Busan, Korea

Brisbane, Australia

Hong Kong

  1. Kyoto, Japan

    2. My favorite city in Japan. I love the culture, the food, the scenery—everything is amazing!

  2. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

    3. One of the most beautiful pieces of architecture in Singapore. It’s even better to be on top of it, drinking and hanging out with friends.

  3. Busan, Korea

    4. We go here once a year for training. They have great food, great culture, and cool tourist spots as well.

  4. Brisbane, Australia

    5. It was so cool to get up close with a kangaroo and take a selfie. I’m surprised by how big they really are in person and how buff they look.

  5. Monster Building, Hong Kong

    6. This building is a popular tourist spot in Hong Kong. The way it’s structured is very unique, and when you stand in the middle of it, the whole thing feels so massive, just like a monster.

Cool Stuff I've Experienced in the Navy

Ronald Raegan, CVN-76

EA18G-Growlers

Airforce KC-10

Refueling Mid Air

Fleet Warships

  • Ronald Raegan, CVN-76

    • Experienced working on a flight deck during flight schedules. It is very cool to see, but it can also get scary and stressful at times.

  • EA18G-Growlers

    • Worked on this bad boy, and became a lead supervisor and quality assurance inspector for this aircraft.

  • Airforce KC-10

    • Rode on this giant cargo plane from Japan to Washington State. It was a very cool experience, but the whole flight was not comfortable at all—and it was very loud.

  • Refueling Mid Air

    • Got to experience and watch how refueling an aircraft mid-air is done. This was a very cool experience!

  • Fleet Warships

    • Got to see a fleet of warships after a RIMPAC exercise. I’m glad it was just an exercise because it was crazy to see so many warships all together.

My Favortite Food While in Japan

These are my favorite go to food while I was stationed in Japan for two and a half years.

Oyakodon
Ramen
Sushi

Chashu Pork
Chashu pork is a Japanese braised pork belly, tender and flavorful, often served in ramen.
Oyakodon
Oyakodon is a Japanese rice bowl topped with chicken, egg, and onions simmered in a savory soy-based sauce.
Ramen
A bowl of rich ramen topped with pork, boiled eggs, and seaweed in a savory broth.
Sushi
Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, seafood, and vegetables.
Takoyaki
Takoyaki is a popular Japanese street food made of small, round balls of batter filled with octopus, topped with sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes.
