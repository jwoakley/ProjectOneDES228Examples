Andrew Scott

• Website

Gian loves Andrew Scott’s art. The way Scott uses the frame and incorporates different components into his work is truly fascinating. Gian appreciates how Scott's art deviates from traditional framed artwork while still being highly effective. The attention to detail in each piece and the consistency throughout Scott's body of work are impressive. When Gian sees his art, it sparks thoughts on how similar concepts can be applied as an aspiring UI designer. It's really about thinking outside the box—or in this case, the frame.

Zander Whitehurst

• Instagram • LinkedIn

Ever since Gian decided to learn and get into UX/UI Design, he has always followed Zander Whitehurst. Zander has taught him many tips and tricks, and his designs are amazing! Gian has used a few of Zander’s concepts or ideas in his own designs as well. Zander might not know it, but he has significantly helped Gian develop his Figma skills.

Will Paterson

• Website • Instagram • Dribble

Gian has always wanted to learn logo design and branding. Will Paterson's work has always been a source of inspiration, and Gian watches his process and tutorials from time to time. Every time Gian does, he tells himself—one day, I’ll make good logos just like him. Additionally, Gian admires Paterson's story about how he became successful as a content creator and went on to own his own agency.
